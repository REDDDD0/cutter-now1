Frequently Asked Questions

General

Cutter Now is a free online tool that allows you to download and trim YouTube videos. You can extract specific portions of videos and save them in MP3 or MP4 format with various quality options.

General

Yes, Cutter Now is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or premium accounts required. All features are available to everyone at no cost.

Technical

No installation required! Cutter Now works entirely in your web browser. Simply paste a YouTube URL and start downloading. It works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Download

We support two main formats:

  • MP4: For video files with various quality options (360p, 720p, 1080p, etc.)
  • MP3: For audio-only files with high-quality sound
Quality

Quality options depend on the original video. Typically available options include:

  • 360p - Good for small file sizes
  • 720p HD - Standard high definition
  • 1080p Full HD - Best video quality
  • Audio-only MP3 - High-quality audio extraction
Technical

After pasting a YouTube URL and getting video information:

  • Use the trimming section to set start and end times
  • Enter times in HH:MM:SS format (e.g., 00:01:30)
  • Use the slider for quick adjustments
  • Click the reset buttons to restore original duration
Download

Download speed depends on several factors:

  • Video length and quality selected
  • Your internet connection speed
  • Server load at the time
  • Trimming processing time for custom segments

Longer videos and higher quality downloads naturally take more time.

Technical

Cutter Now works on all modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Opera

Make sure JavaScript is enabled for full functionality.

Legal

Downloading YouTube videos should comply with:

  • YouTube's Terms of Service
  • Copyright laws in your country
  • Fair use policies

We recommend downloading only content you own or have permission to use. Use this tool responsibly and respect creators' rights.

Technical

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check if the YouTube URL is correct and accessible
  • Ensure the video is not private or region-locked
  • Try refreshing the page and pasting the URL again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser
  • Check your internet connection

If problems persist, contact our support team.

General

No, we do not store any videos on our servers. All processing is done temporarily, and files are automatically deleted after download. We respect your privacy and do not keep any personal data or downloaded content.

Download

Currently, Cutter Now processes one video at a time. For playlists, you'll need to download each video individually by pasting each URL separately. This ensures better quality control and faster processing for each download.
